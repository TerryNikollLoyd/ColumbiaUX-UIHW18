My UX Skills

This is my design thinking process

Below are three different ways I apply my UX skill set

Research

Lets find out about your audience

Through qualitative and quantitative data gathering I am able to then synthesize that data, and analyze what your site audience is experiencing. I then highlight their goals and painpoints and brainstorm design solutions.

Empathy

Having a end user point of view

Being able to empathize with users of all backgrounds is a skill that is hard to teach and usually comes from years of experience. Empathy is imperative to collecting unbiased data and desigining solutions that will infact benefit your end user.

Solutions

Success we have a plan!

Finding a solution to your goals to better the experience of your users and therefore increase satisfaction with your brand and site is a huge win. UX is the first step to design what that success looks like and begin implementing the design solution.
